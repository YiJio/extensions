noto maki

Extension
  • Add to Chrome (v1.2.0)
  1. Install extension to Chrome.
  2. Click on the noto maki icon.
  3. Start typing to-do items on the space!
  4. Bold, italic, underline, and indentation are available.
  5. Create, rename, and delete tabs & notes.
  6. Customize tab colors, background note types, and completion marks.
  7. Change UI localization to English, Japanese, or Chinese.
  8. Always remember to checkmark your to-dos for completion, that's the point!
